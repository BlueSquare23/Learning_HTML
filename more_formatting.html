More Formatting

Special Characters

Clubs is the character code for a clubs symbol - ♣ or ♣

Dagger is the character code for a dagger symbol - † or †

Here are some other character codes,

lt or 60 for, <

gt or 62 for, >

amp or 38 for, &

copy or 169 for, ©

raquo or 187 for, »

frac12 or 189 for, ½

Preformatting

Sometimes you may want text to appear as is on a page

To do this you want to use the pre tag

For example,

 
This             is    a        sentence
    with some      bizarre formatting.

Comments

Comments can be useful durring development to note potential changes. They can also be useful to note what a particular section of code or writing is for. If you're good to your future self you'll leave comments.
